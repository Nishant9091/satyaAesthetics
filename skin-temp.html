5D FACIAL

5D Skin Revival Facial Treatment at Satya Skin & Hair Clinic in Delhi is a truly unique and highly effective medi-facial. It is a combination of the most useful technologies for skin care.

WHY DO WE NEED MEDICAL
5D FACICAL TREATMENTS?

Our skin is the outermost shield which has to deal with all kinds of environmental stressors like the sunlight, extremes of temperature, pollution and humidity. These factors often result in skin issues like abnormal pigmentation, enlarged pores, fine lines, dull lustreless skin, etc.

The medical facial treatments like the ‘5D Skin Revival Facial treatment’’ at Satya Skin and Hair Clinic are scientifically crafted to address these problems and would help to regain the lost radiance and youthfulness of your skin.

HOW FREQUENTLY
SHOULD IT BE DONE?

A 5D Skin Revival facial treatment is recommended every 4 weeks for the first 3-4 sessions by our skin doctors. This is to give an initial push to hasten the recovery of the damaged skin. Later on, we recommend continuing this medi-facial every 8 weeks to maintain the results.
